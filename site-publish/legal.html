Incubation

Apache Pivot is an effort undergoing incubation at The ASF, sponsored by the Apache Incubator Project Management Committee (PMC). Incubation is required of all newly accepted projects until a further review indicates that the infrastructure, communications, and decision making process have stabilized in a manner consistent with other successful ASF projects. While incubation status is not necessarily a reflection of the completeness or stability of the code, it does indicate that the project has yet to be fully endorsed by the ASF.
